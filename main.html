Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a drama film based on the novella by Stephen King. It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence. While in prison, Andy forms a bond with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and works to gain his freedom.
